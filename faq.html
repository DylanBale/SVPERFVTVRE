Frequently Asked Questions

Which AI is best for coding?

For coding tasks, we typically recommend Cursor for full project development, GitHub Copilot for inline code completion, and Claude for explaining code and debugging complex issues. ChatGPT is also excellent for quick code snippets.

What's the difference between ChatGPT and Claude?

ChatGPT excels at multimodal tasks (images, voice, vision) and has broader integrations. Claude is preferred for longer writing tasks, nuanced editing, and conversations that require careful reasoning. Many users subscribe to both.

Which AI should I use for image generation?

Midjourney produces the most artistic, stylized images. DALL-E (via ChatGPT) is better for photorealistic images and now offers editing capabilities. For quick mockups and marketing assets, ChatGPT's built-in image generation is very convenient.

Is there a free AI I can use?

Grok offers the best free tier with deep research, reasoning, and image generation. ChatGPT and Claude also have free tiers with limited usage. Ollama lets you run AI models completely free on your own computer.

Which AI is best for research?

Perplexity is purpose-built for research with cited sources. ChatGPT's Deep Research mode provides comprehensive reports. Grok has real-time access to X/Twitter for current events.

Which AI is best for writing and editing?

Claude is widely considered the best for writing tasks. It adapts to your style, provides thoughtful edits, and excels at longer documents. ChatGPT is better for quick drafts and brainstorming, while Gemini handles very long documents well.

Can AI models process PDFs and documents?

Yes! Gemini has the largest context window (2M+ tokens) and can process entire books. Claude also handles long documents well. ChatGPT can analyze PDFs and images. For local/private document processing, Ollama keeps everything on your machine.

Which AI has the most up-to-date information?

Perplexity and Grok have real-time web access. Grok uniquely has access to X/Twitter posts. ChatGPT and Claude have knowledge cutoffs but can browse the web in some modes. For breaking news and current events, Perplexity or Grok are your best options.
